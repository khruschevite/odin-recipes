Apple, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie

Description

Smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup vanilla almond milk
  • 1 apple - peeled, cored, and cubed
  • 1 cup halved frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup halved frozen strawberries
  • 1/4 cup vanilla Greek yogurt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Place almond milk, apple, strawberries, banana, and yogurt in a blender, being sure the milk goes in first. Process until smooth, occasionally scraping down the sides of the blender with a rubber spatula, if necessary.
  2. Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.
